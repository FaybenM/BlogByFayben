Maximalist

I have seen the slow progression of getting out of the millenial grey style to the maximalist style and I have been loving it. I love the colorful aspect of maximalism, with touches of darker tones throughout the room. It gives a fun and artsy feel to the home. It also reminds me of the early 2000s! I loved watching Lizzie Mcguire and That's so Raven and they both had distinct styles when it came to their bedrooms and just their overall aestheitc. I'm glad to be a little bit older now and be able to divulge myself into that style for my younger self.

Organic modern

When scrolling through pinterest I have come across some home decor that give off a neutral tone and clean look to it. I looked up what that was called and the organic modern aesthetic popped up. This is such a complete 180 from the maximalism decor, but I love how bright the rooms look and it gives a sense of coziness. I love fireplaces so I can imagine a nice cozy fireplace in the living room with some touches of wood accents in the room and a velvet couch. It also makes it look a bit classy, yet still homey. I also love when a room gets amazing sunlight and with this type of house vibe you can get big windows that bring in great lighting throughout the house.
